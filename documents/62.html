The following is a timeline of the presidency of Barack Obama, from January 1, 2015 to the present. For his time as President-elect, see the Presidential transition of Barack Obama; for a detailed account of his first months in office, see First 100 days of Barack Obama's presidency; and for a complete itinerary of his travels, see List of presidential trips made by Barack Obama.

January[edit]

  • January 6 – President Obama holds a bilateral meeting with President Enrique Peña Nieto of Mexico.
  • January 16 – President Obama holds a bilateral meeting and a press conference with Prime Minister David Cameron of the United Kingdom.
  • January 22 - President Obama holds a live interview with YouTube creators Bethany Mota, GloZell Green and Hank Green.

February[edit]

  • February 3 – President Obama holds a bilateral meeting with King Abdullah II of Jordan.
  • February 9 – President Obama holds a bilateral meeting and a press conference with Chancellor Angela Merkel of Germany.
